About our town:

Máximo Paz is located in the south of Santa Fe province, Argentina, next to Middle Creek (the geographical limit that separates Santa Fe from Buenos Aires province and is referred to as the “arch of the boot”). Founded in 1890 by Marcelo Paz, our town is where our company was born and developed.
